Zenith is a product development exercise and a nifty 3D printer exhaust filtering system.

It previously was intended to be a whole-room purifier with smart capabilities, but it was scrubbed in favor of the simpler yet more necessary 3D printer implementation.

## Specs
I haven't tested Zenith properly yet, nor do I have the necessary equipment, but when I do I'll post the sweet sweet data here. For now, you get some basic technical specifications:

- Runs on 24 VDC
- Uses MERV filters
- ~40-45 CFM
- ~2 mmH2O
- ~26 dB
